Help guide for OfficeManager

In the OfficeManager program you can do four main functions:

  1. Type 'add' to add an employee to your list of employees. The preexisting schedule is always 9am - 5pm, Monday-Friday. You can change this later with the 'edit' function.
  2. Type 'remove' to remove an employee and their schedule from the list.
  3. Type 'edit' to edit the schedule of an employee. Here you can change the days they work, and the times that they work for each day.
  4. Type 'print' to see your full list of employees and their corresponding schedules.

Help guide for CustomerManager

In the CustomerManager program you can do four main functions:

  1. Type 'add' to add a customer to your list of employees. By entering a customer into the system, you can keep their contact information, keep track of when they do business with you, and keep info about what they do with your business.
  2. Type 'edit' to edit the info of a customer.
  3. Type 'remove' to remove a customer and any info about them from the list.
  4. Type 'print' to see a full list of customers. You can then enter a customer's' name to see their information.
